Our Menu

Chicken

Our signature chicken dishes feature farm-fresh, free-range poultry prepared with aromatic herbs and spices. From crispy fried chicken with our secret blend of seasonings to tender grilled chicken breast marinated in lemon and rosemary, each dish is cooked to perfection. Try our popular honey-glazed chicken wings or the classic chicken parmesan served with fresh basil and melted mozzarella.

Beef

Premium cuts of beef sourced from local ranches and aged to perfection. Our beef selection includes juicy ribeye steaks grilled to your preference, slow-braised beef short ribs that fall off the bone, and our famous beef wellington wrapped in flaky pastry. Each dish is seasoned with fresh herbs and paired with roasted vegetables and signature sauces.

Sushi

Experience authentic Japanese cuisine with our fresh sushi selection made daily by our master sushi chef. Our menu features premium sashimi-grade fish including salmon, tuna, and yellowtail, along with specialty rolls like our signature dragon roll and spicy tuna roll. All sushi is served with wasabi, pickled ginger, and our house-made soy sauce blend for the perfect flavor experience.
